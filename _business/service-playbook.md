# Speed of Now — SMB Digital Recovery Service Playbook

> Status: BUILDING — add steps as the A Custom Coach project progresses.

## Service Overview

Digital recovery and modernization for established local businesses with outdated or broken web presence.

## Target Client Profile

- Business established 10+ years
- Owner is 50+ and doesn't want to learn Squarespace
- Has revenue, will pay for professional work
- Current site is outdated, broken, or nonexistent
- No one on staff handles digital

## Pricing Tiers

| Tier | Price | Includes |
|------|-------|----------|
| Starter | $3,000–$5,000 | 5-6 page site, basic SEO, deployment |
| Professional | $5,000–$8,000 | Full rebuild, email migration, SEO foundation |
| Premium | $8,000–$15,000 | Custom site, email, SEO, booking system, content, photography direction |
| Monthly retainer | $150–$500/mo | Hosting, updates, SEO maintenance, support |

## Process (Document Each Step as You Do It for John)

1. Discovery call
2. Content audit / recovery
3. Competitive landscape review
4. Design direction + client approval
5. Build (Next.js + Tailwind + Vercel)
6. Content writing / refinement
7. Booking system integration
8. SEO foundation
9. Email migration (Google Workspace)
10. Domain security (auto-renew, transfer lock)
11. Directory cleanup (consistent NAP)
12. Client walkthrough + handoff
13. Monthly retainer kickoff
